What is Urban Science?

Urban science is the research of cities, urbanisation, and the integrated use of data analysis, technology, design and policy to provide solutions to make our cities more liveable, resilient and sustainable.

>What is Urban Analytics?

Urban analytics is the application of a combination of data mining, machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques to gain insight into, and address major issues that cities face.

>What is Urban Planning?

Urban planning is a social, political and technical process focused on the development and design of land use and the built environment in urban domains, to maintain the health and social well-being of humans while maintaining sustainability goals.
