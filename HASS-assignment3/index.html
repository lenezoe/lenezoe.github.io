The Value of HDB Lease Decay

All HDB flats built in Singapore have a 99-year or shorter leasehold. There has been much public debate over the value of HDB flats as retirement or depreciating assets [Examples: 1, 2, 3].

Does the remaining lease period of HDB flats really have a strong influence on their resale prices? In the following visualisations (grouped scatter plots), I explore the relationship between remaining flat leases and average resale flat prices , in mature and non-mature towns. I am also interested in the influence of remaining flat lease on average resale flat prices between different flat types (coloured clusters).
